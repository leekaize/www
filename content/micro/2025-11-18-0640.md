[Why you feel out of control?](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/28M9LQakitM) Most people in their 20s face foreclosure: committing without exploring alternatives. They compensate with external pressure—high-stakes jobs, constant deadlines—but this creates productivity without purpose since foreclosed identities rely on external rather than internal motivation. Real achievement requires exploration first.
