title: 100% daily
date: 2025-11-24T16:19:00

Perform at 100% effort and quality for seven days = 700 high quality output. Hustle at 120% for five, crash for two = 600—you lose. [Fatigue compounds](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/public-health/articles/10.3389/fpubh.2023.1193942/full) daily until Friday you're forcing 120% from a 70% baseline with poor quality—[conditioning yourself throughout the day](https://www.patriciabannan.com/blog/health/the-power-of-micro-breaks-for-well-being-and-work-performance/) to stay at 100%.
